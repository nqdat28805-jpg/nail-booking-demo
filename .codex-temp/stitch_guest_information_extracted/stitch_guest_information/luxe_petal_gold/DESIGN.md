# Design System Document

## Theme Overview

This document outlines the core visual theme for our application, focusing on a harmonious blend of classic and modern aesthetics.

## Color Palette

Our palette is carefully selected to evoke a sense of warmth and sophistication, with a light default color mode.

*   **Primary Color:** `#D9A2A2` - A soft, inviting red-brown that serves as the brand's signature. Used for key interactive elements like buttons and primary calls to action.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#B89352` - A warm, golden tan that complements the primary color, used for secondary actions and subtle highlights.
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#9E8071` - A muted, earthy brown providing an additional accent for decorative elements and badges.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#F9F5F2` - A very light, almost off-white for backgrounds and general UI surfaces, ensuring readability and a clean look.

## Typography

A thoughtful combination of serif and sans-serif fonts ensures both elegance and clarity across the interface.

*   **Headlines:** `notoSerif` - Provides a classic, sophisticated touch for titles and prominent text.
*   **Body Text:** `plusJakartaSans` - A modern, highly legible sans-serif for all general body content, ensuring a comfortable reading experience.
*   **Labels:** `plusJakartaSans` - Consistent with body text for form labels and smaller UI elements, maintaining clarity.

## Shape and Form

The interaction between elements is defined by subtle rounding and a balanced approach to spacing.

*   **Roundedness:** `2` - UI elements feature a moderate level of roundedness, striking a balance between modern softness and structured form.
*   **Spacing:** `2` - A normal level of spacing ensures a clean, organized layout without feeling overly dense or sparse. This provides good visual separation and readability.
